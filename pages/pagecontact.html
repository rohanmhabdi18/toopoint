Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by visiting "My Bookings" section in your account or by contacting our support team. Cancellation policies vary by provider.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after cancellation approval. The exact timing depends on your payment method and bank.

Can I modify my booking details?

Most bookings can be modified subject to availability and provider policies. Check your booking confirmation email or contact support for assistance.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions. You'll receive a reset link via email.
